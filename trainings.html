03

Energy Management

Sustainable performance. Burnout prevention as a team operating system.

+
For

Leaders, managers, and teams at risk of burning out the people who hold the work. Helping professions, high-load operators, and anyone navigating sustained change.

Formats

1-hour briefing. 4-week series (4–8 hours total). Half-day workshop or full-day intensive.

Outcomes
  • The 4 Domains of Energy: physical, emotional, mental, purpose
  • A personal or team energy audit
  • Early signs of burnout and cognitive overload
  • One operational system redesigned to reduce cognitive load
  • Sustainable boundaries and a 30-day energy plan
04

Psychology of Motivation

Understand intrinsic and extrinsic motivation, and build the environments where people want to perform.

+
For

Leadership teams, managers, and culture-builders responsible for engagement, retention, and performance. Strong fit during change, hybrid transitions, or post-merger culture work.

Formats

1-hour briefing. Half-day workshop. 4-week series for leadership cohorts. Custom team workshop.

Outcomes
  • The difference between motivation and compliance, and why incentives often produce the wrong one
  • The four conditions for real engagement: autonomy, mastery, purpose, and identity fit
  • A diagnostic for whether your team is motivated by fear, recognition, or alignment
  • Practical levers leaders can pull to build motivating environments
  • (Multi-week only) A team motivation audit and a 30-day environment redesign plan
