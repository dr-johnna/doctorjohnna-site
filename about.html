About me. I designed my life. For Purpose and Peace.

I'm Dr. Johnna Richardson, PhD. A Business Strategist and AI Consultant with a doctorate in psychology. I help people build from who they actually are.

The story.

I grew up a missionary kid and traveled to more than thirty countries. It let me see identity in a way most people don't, long before I had words for it. Who we are is built from our internal structure, not only external environments.

Three of my own businesses, built over twenty years. Each one started the moment my identity caught up to who I was becoming. The hard parts built the frameworks.

The first was a nonprofit, founded in 2008, using therapeutic photography with at-risk youth. I ran programs across South Carolina, Bangladesh, Kansas City, and Florida. That work taught me what I still teach: information matters, but perspective comes first. People have to see themselves differently before the rest takes hold.

The second was an international photography business, fifteen years telling the stories of thousands of people. I revealed their beautiful truth and helped them see it for themselves.

The third is the work I do now. Identity-based brand strategy and AI consulting for founders and mission-driven organizations.

Underneath all of it, twenty years as a therapist and trauma specialist, and ten years teaching psychology at the university level. That work taught me how people actually heal and change, and it grounds everything I build now.

All three run on the same engine. Psychology, identity, and the work of building from the inside out.

What I believe.

Six values I run my life and work on. Peace, purpose, and the freedom to spend my time on what matters.

  • Peace

    Always protect your peace through safety: physically, mentally, and emotionally.

  • Purpose

    Knowing your purpose and living a purpose-fueled life is key to personal and professional fulfillment.

  • Value

    When you know your worth, you invite others to know theirs.

  • Grit

    Grit is perseverance you keep choosing, especially through the hard seasons.

  • Play

    Understanding your play personality and making time for play and creative expression.

  • Rest

    Rest increases productivity, creativity, and renews your vision.

Where I am now.

Today the work happens from quiet Big Pine Key, in the Florida Keys. I live here with my husband, Erik, a cancer survivor whose journey has deepened everything I teach about resilience. Our dog, Leyla, loves our boat days, exploring clear waters and hidden beaches.

Peace, the ocean at our doorstep, and work that means something. By God's grace, that's the life I designed.
