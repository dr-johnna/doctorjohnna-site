Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this website, you agree to this Policy.

Who we are

This website is operated by Dr. Johnna Consulting, LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), located at 29959 Overseas Hwy, #430431, Big Pine Key, FL 33043, accessible at doctorjohnna.com.

What we collect

We keep our data collection minimal. We collect:

Information you give us directly. When you fill out a form on our website, we collect your name and email address. If you contact us, we collect whatever information you choose to include in your message.

Basic usage information. Like most websites, our site and the services that host it may automatically receive standard technical information when you visit, such as your browser type, the pages you view, the date and time of your visit, and similar diagnostic data. We use this to keep the site working and to understand general traffic patterns.

If you purchase from us, your payment is processed by Stripe, and your payment details are collected and handled directly by Stripe under its own privacy policy. We do not collect or store your full payment card information.

What we do not collect

We do not run member logins or accounts on this website. We do not ask for your phone number or mailing address through the website. We collect only what is described in this Policy.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide the services, programs, and products you request.
  • Send you emails you have signed up to receive, including updates, content, and offers.
  • Process purchases through our payment processor.
  • Maintain, protect, and improve the website.
  • Comply with our legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information.

Email and marketing

We use MailerLite to manage our email list and send communications. If you sign up, you will receive emails from us, and you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email. When you unsubscribe, we stop sending marketing emails, though we may retain a record as needed for our records and legal obligations.

Service providers

We share information only with the third-party tools that help us operate, and only as needed for them to perform their function. These currently include MailerLite (email), Stripe (payments), and Skool (community and program delivery). Each of these processes data under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies.

Cookies and analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies for two purposes. Some are necessary for the site to function. Others help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

We use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, to collect standard information about how visitors interact with our website, such as which pages are viewed, how long visits last, and general location and device information. Google Analytics uses cookies and may collect your IP address. This information is processed in a way that does not directly identify you, and we use it only to understand overall traffic patterns and improve the site. Google processes this data under its own privacy policy, and you can learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/privacy.

You can set your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent, though some parts of the site may not work properly if you do. You can also opt out of Google Analytics across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this Policy, or as required to meet our legal obligations, after which we delete or stop using it.

Your choices and rights

You can unsubscribe from our emails at any time. You can also contact us to ask what information we hold about you, to correct it, or to request that we delete it. We will honor those requests to the extent required by applicable law, keeping only what we are legally required to retain.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is fully secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

This website is intended for adults and is not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us information, please contact us and we will remove it.

Third-party links

Our website may link to other sites we do not control. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices, and we encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted, and the "Last updated" date will reflect the most recent version.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to us at doctorjohnna.com/contact.
